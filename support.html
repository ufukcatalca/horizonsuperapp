Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, open the app and tap on "Sign Up" on the login screen. Follow the prompts to enter your information and create your account.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login screen, tap on "Forgot Password". Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions on how to reset your password.

How often is the content updated?

Our content is updated regularly to ensure you have access to the most current information. Updates are typically made on a weekly basis.

How can I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues with the app, please use the contact form above to report them. Include as much detail as possible about the issue, including steps to reproduce it.
